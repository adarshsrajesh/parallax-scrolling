Vortex of Valour

In the cursed village of Tsukiyama, darkness has become eternal. The villagers live in fear as the Yokai, undead warlords bound by ancient magic, rise nightly to torment them. Hope rests on the legend of a powerful warrior destined to wield the Blade of the Eternal Dawn, a weapon with the strength to end the Yokai's hold.

The prophecy calls forth Takashi, a legendary samurai whose past is shrouded in mystery. Armed with the mystical blade, he begins his journey to face the Yokai lords, each commanding unique powers drawn from the elements—fire, ice, thunder, and shadow. With each battle, Takashi discovers hidden truths about the curse and unearths pieces of an ancient ritual tying the Yokai to the village’s fate. Along the way, he encounters a few brave villagers willing to help, some revealing long-lost knowledge and guidance for Takashi’s quest..

As the samurai moves through haunted forests, ancient shrines, and decayed landscapes, he must make choices that reveal his true nature. Each choice affects the people of Tsukiyama and the land itself, determining who he can trust, what secrets remain hidden, and ultimately, if he has the strength to finish his mission.

The deeper Takashi goes, the closer he comes to a final, life-altering revelation. Whether Tsukiyama will see light again lies in his hands—and the player's.

Gameplay Highlights

Intense Combat: Master the art of samurai swordplay with a variety of combat techniques.

Mythic Battles: Face the Yokai lords in battles that push Takashi’s skill and resolve to the limit.

Environmental Exploration: Uncover secrets and legends through richly detailed locations steeped in mystery and danger.

Moral Choices: Every decision influences the story and leads to different possible endings, placing the fate of Tsukiyama and Takashi’s soul in the player’s control..
